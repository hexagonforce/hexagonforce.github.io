Things that I support

STAR Voting

STAR Voting is an alternative voting method that allows all citizens to vote their conscience and still get results that are closest to the aggregate vote of the citizens. It is an extension of Range Voting that makes good compromises to answer common criticisms against Range Voting (such as the Unknown Lunatic nightmare).
